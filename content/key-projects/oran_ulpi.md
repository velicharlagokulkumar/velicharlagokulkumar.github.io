ORAN ULPI (Uplink Performance Improvement) is a technology for Open RAN that boosts Massive MIMO efficiency. By utilizing the 7.2x (Cat-B) split, it moves complex beamforming tasks to the O-RU. This significantly slashes fronthaul data demands and costs while maintaining high-performance processing.
